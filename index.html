About Me

Hi, I'm Mary, and I started this because of a feeling I couldn't shake.

When I became a mother, I noticed something that broke my heart a little. There were hundreds of photos of my baby, but almost none of us together. Not because we didn't have moments worth remembering, but because I was always the one behind the phone. No one was taking good photos of me with my child.

Time moves so fast. Those sleepy newborn days, the first giggles, the tiny hands wrapped around your finger. Blink and they're gone. I never want another mother to look back and realize she has no images of herself in those moments.

That's why I picked up a camera. I'm a photographer based in Downey, serving families across Los Angeles and the surrounding communities. I specialize in families, newborns, and the beautiful chaos of motherhood — and I'm here so you can actually be in the photos, not just take them.
